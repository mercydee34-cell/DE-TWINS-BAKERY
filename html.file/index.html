Our Story

Welcome to DE TWINS BREAD, where our passion for baking has been a family tradition for generations. We believe that great bread starts with the simplest, highest-quality ingredients—flour, water, yeast, and salt. Our commitment is to craft artisan bread with traditional techniques, ensuring every loaf is a masterpiece of flavor and texture. We are dedicated to bringing you the freshest bread, baked daily and made with no preservatives or artificial flavors. From our kitchen to your table, we promise a taste of pure, wholesome goodness.

We are excited to take bread creation to an amazing new level, introducing more forms and new styles of bread for you to enjoy. This passion and dedication is led by our C.E.O.s, **Dauda Taiwo and Dauda Kehinde**. We invite you to experience the difference for yourself and enjoy your delighted day!
